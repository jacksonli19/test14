Objective

A reliable learning hub for difficult courses.

The objective of this website is to provide accessible academic resources, structured study notes, and future video tutorials for students at Hostos Community College.

As a tutor at the Hostos Academic Learning Centre (HALC), my goal is to help peers master challenging concepts in mathematics, history, and economics while building long-term academic confidence.

About

Hi, I'm Jackson.

Welcome to my academic portfolio and resource hub. I graduated from Hunter College in the Spring of 2026 with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics, a minor in Mathematics and Business Studies.

My journey with Hostos Community College began as a student through ePermit courses, and later continued when I began working as a tutor at the Hostos Academic Learning Centre in Fall 2025.

This website is an extension of my work at the centre, designed to provide structured notes, study guides, and upcoming video resources to help you build confidence and succeed in your coursework.

You're welcome to contact me should you have any questions. Likewise, if you spot a mistake that needs correcting, want to say thank you, or just want to get in contact, feel free to send me an email at jackson.li19@myhunter.cuny.edu.

All subject pages currently feature comprehensive study notes, and recorded video tutorials will be added in the future to walk through complex problems step by step.
