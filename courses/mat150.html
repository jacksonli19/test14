College Algebra with Trigonometric Functions

Course scenario

MAT 150 is the foundation course for algebraic modelling, functions, trigonometry, and the problem-solving habits students need before moving into calculus. This page collects the study material in one plain reading flow so it feels like an article page rather than a separate app screen.

Use these notes to review lecture ideas, prepare for exams, and revisit worked examples. The resources are free for personal study through the Hostos Academic Learning Centre.
